What is CodeUpLoader?

Throughout the course cycle valuable information to supplement the Codeup curriculum was posted via Slack and Zoom. However, the information was not persistent and students are unable to retrieve it because neither platform has a method to view past history.

CodeUpLoader is a tool designed to capture and retain information and files shared with Codeup students. It allows users to create a “resource” containing the shared information, including the capability to upload text and/or files, and save them to a database ensuring persistence.
