As graduates of CodeUp, we are looking forward to the next chapter of our lives. To leave CodeUp better than we found it, we created CodeUpLoader. Finally, students and instructors can come together to share their knowledge and resources in a more organized, performant, persistent manner.

What makes our Capstone special

Persistent data

Class related materials are stored in a fully CRUD-able database with no expiration date to speak of. Students can access their course-related materials at any time from an internal, organized, and searchable database.

Slack integration

Just can't leave Slack behind? No problem! CodeUpLoader is fully integrated with Slack, allowing alerts to be pushed to your members whenever a new resource is posted.

Connects the dots

Clearly see what lesson(s) the shared resource is related to per the course curriculum with a direct link to java.codeup; no need go to the website and dig it out, interrupting your workflow.
